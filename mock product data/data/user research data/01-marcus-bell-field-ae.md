| | |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Field Account Executive (enterprise logistics) |
| **Account** | Atlas Freight — Enterprise plan |
| **Region** | Americas |

| **Devices** | Phone all day; laptop only at home in the evening |
| **Quota attainment** | 118% — a top performer |

## A day in Marcus's life
Marcus is on the road by 7:30am, visiting 4–6 shipping and warehouse clients across the
state. Between site visits he sits in his truck and **opens HeroForce on his phone to see
which deals are heating up and what's next** — he does this 3–4 times a day, every day.
He almost never sits at a desk during working hours.

What he **doesn't** do is update anything while he's out. After a client meeting he's
already driving to the next one; typing a call summary, changing the deal stage, and
filling in next-step fields on a phone "isn't happening." He tells himself he'll do it
Friday afternoon — and about half the time he actually does.

## In his words
> "I check my deals on my phone between meetings all day — but there's no way I'm typing a
> call summary into all those fields while I'm walking into the next one. I do it Friday,
> if I remember."

## Observations
- Opens HeroForce on his phone 3–4 times a day to see which deals are heating up; almost
  never at a desk during working hours.
- Doesn't update anything while he's out — defers logging to Friday, and follows through
  about half the time.
- Would benefit from fast, mobile-friendly logging he can do between visits.
