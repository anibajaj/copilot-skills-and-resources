# User Profile — Diego Alvarez

> DEMO persona — an illustrative HeroForce user-research profile.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Territory Rep (field sales) |
| **Account** | Delmar Financial — Growth plan |
| **Region** | LATAM |
| **Tenure** | 3 years on HeroForce |
| **Devices** | Phone (mobile-only during the day) |
| **Work setting** | In the car / at client sites ~80% of the day |

## A day in Diego's life
Diego covers a wide territory and spends most of the day driving between client offices. He
uses HeroForce **on his phone, from the car**, to check his route of accounts and see which
deals need attention before he walks in. He's a daily viewer — the app is his pre-meeting
briefing.

Updating from the road is a non-starter for him: it's not safe to type while driving, and
after a meeting he's already moving to the next stop. He genuinely *wants* the CRM to be
current — his manager pushes him on it — but the only realistic window is late at night,
and by then he's forgotten the details.

## In his words
> "I'm in my car or at client sites most of the day. I open HeroForce to see my pipeline,
> but I can't safely update a deal from the road. Give me a voice note and be done."

## Observations
- Mobile-only during the day; checks his pipeline from the car before each client visit.
- Can't safely update from the road; the only realistic window is late at night, by which
  point he's forgotten the details.
- Explicitly asks for hands-free / voice logging.
