# User Profile — Priya Nair

> DEMO persona — an illustrative HeroForce user-research profile.

| | |
|---|---|
| **Role** | Inside Sales Rep (high-volume phone sales) |
| **Account** | Bluewave Retail — Growth plan |
| **Region** | Americas |
| **Tenure** | 2 years on HeroForce |
| **Devices** | Desktop (headset) during calls; phone after hours |
| **Daily calls** | 45–60 dials, back-to-back |

## A day in Priya's life
Priya lives on the phone. Her day is a wall of back-to-back calls with almost no gaps. She
keeps HeroForce open on a second monitor and **glances at the deal record while the line is
ringing** — she can see who she's calling, the last note, the amount. That glance happens
dozens of times a day.

But the moment a call ends, the next one begins. **Stopping to log the call, update the
stage, and set a follow-up would eat the 20 seconds she has before the next dial**, so she
doesn't. She promises herself she'll "clean it up at the end of the day," but by 6pm she's
drained and the backlog is 50 calls deep.

## In her words
> "I'm on back-to-back calls. I can glance at the deal while I'm dialing, but stopping to
> log every call and update the stage? No time. It piles up."

## Observations
- Keeps a deal record open on a second monitor and glances at it dozens of times a day
  while dialing.
- Back-to-back calls leave no time to log; the update backlog piles up and often isn't
  cleared by end of day.
- Wants logging that takes seconds, not minutes — ideally auto-drafted from the call so
  she just confirms.
