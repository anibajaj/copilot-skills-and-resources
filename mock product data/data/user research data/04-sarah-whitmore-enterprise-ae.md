## A day in Sarah's life
Sarah runs a small number of large, high-touch enterprise deals. Her calendar is wall-to-
wall external meetings and calls with procurement, clinical, and legal stakeholders. She
**opens HeroForce every morning to review deal status and check what AI Assist flagged**,
and dips in between meetings to see where things stand.

Because her meetings are dense and confidential, she doesn't update the record live. She
does a **weekly "CRM hygiene" block on Sunday evening** to bring everything current — which
means for six days a week she's a viewer, not an updater. When Sundays get skipped, deals
go stale even though she's actively working them.

## In her words
> "The app is great for seeing what's next. Updating it is the problem — it feels like
> paperwork I do after hours, not something I can do live."

## Observations
- Opens HeroForce every morning and between meetings to review deal status and check what

- Doesn't update live during dense, confidential meetings; batches everything into a
